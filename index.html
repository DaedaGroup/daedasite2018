About Daeda Group

Daeda is a team of developers inspired to develop modern intuitive web projects to suit your needs.

We've been supporting major brands developing web applications for over fifteen years. Our team consists of different cultures with a common passion for evolving technology and engineering.
We're a group of friends working in the development and marketing industry to help define a better way to deliver fast and effective solutions that produce top results.

Services

  • UX/UI Design

We do our best to understand what the users need and deliver that information. Our designers look for the clear path to get our users to the end result by providing a clear and consistent interface to ensure the least amount of effort required when using the product.
We try to get a clear picture of what needs will bring people to your website/app, and exactly how it will go about providing a design that will draw them in further.

Modularity is the way of the future.

We can help architect your product's infrastructure to be scalable to your business' needs, while making sure your product's integrity and security is not compromised.
We develop with one philosophy in mind: KISS (Keep It Simple Stupid). No one would want spaghetti code with tomato sauce full of bugs. By keeping things simple, we can help eliminate most common development issues.

One mobile technology to rule them all

Nothing is worse than having to do the same thing twice. We use cutting edge technologies for mobile development that will save you both time and money.
Our expertise in the mobile industry since its inception allows us to assist you in your mobile application's needs.
